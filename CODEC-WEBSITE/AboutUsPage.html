About CODEC

Discover the story behind our coding club and what drives us to create a community of passionate developers

Our Journey

[2025]

Expanded to 10 international chapters with 5000+ active members globally. Launched CODEC Innovation Labs for advanced research projects.

[2022]

Established first industry partnerships program connecting students with leading tech companies. Hosted inaugural CODEC Tech Conference.

[2019]

Launched mentorship program and organized first inter-college coding competition with 200+ participants from 15 colleges.

[2017]

CODEC was founded by a group of passionate computer science students aiming to create a collaborative space for coding enthusiasts. Started with weekly coding meetups and peer learning sessions.

Our Vision

Empowering Innovators

To create a global community of developers who use technology to solve real-world problems and drive positive change through innovation.

Universal Access

Making coding education accessible to everyone regardless of background, with free resources and mentorship programs.

Our Mission

Community Building

Foster a supportive environment where developers collaborate, share knowledge, and grow together.

Skill Development

Provide hands-on learning experiences through workshops, hackathons, and real-world projects.

Our Culture

Collaboration First

We believe in the power of teamwork and collective problem-solving.

Continuous Learning

Always curious, always growing - we embrace lifelong learning.
